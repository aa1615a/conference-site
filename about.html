What is Social 360?

Social 360 is a three-day conference about virtual reality, augmented reality, 360 video and how they can create impactful social change.

This will be the conference's inagural year and our entire team has high hopes for the positive change that can be happen by sharing knowlege and passions. The technology we work with had the potential to do incredible things. We hope this conference will show all of the good that it can do and has already done.

10% of profit from the ticket sales will be donated to One Laptop Per Child, a charity that provides low cost, low power laptops to children around the world.

Thank you to all of our crowdfunding donors. This conference would not be possible with you. Thank you also to our incredible speakers. They have been exceptionally supportive every step of the way.
